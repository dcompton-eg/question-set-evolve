# Interview Question Set Design Prompt

## Role Being Hired For
Senior Software Engineer - Backend Systems

## Context
We are hiring for a senior backend engineer position at a mid-sized technology company.
The role involves:
- Designing and implementing scalable backend services
- Working with distributed systems and databases
- Mentoring junior engineers
- Participating in system design decisions
- Writing clean, maintainable code

## Interview Format
- 60-minute technical interview
- Mix of technical and behavioral questions
- Focus on practical experience and problem-solving

## Candidate Level
Senior (5+ years experience expected)

## Skills to Assess
1. **System Design**: Ability to design scalable, reliable systems
2. **Problem Solving**: Analytical thinking and debugging skills
3. **Technical Depth**: Deep knowledge in backend technologies
4. **Communication**: Ability to explain complex concepts clearly
5. **Collaboration**: Experience working in teams
6. **Growth Mindset**: Learning ability and adaptability

## Question Requirements
- 4-6 questions total
- Include at least one system design question
- Include at least one behavioral question
- Include at least one coding/algorithm question
- Appropriate for 60-minute interview
- Questions should build in complexity

## Constraints
- Questions should not require specific company knowledge
- Avoid questions that have well-known "textbook" answers
- Ensure questions allow for discussion and follow-ups
- Questions should be answerable without a whiteboard/IDE

## Output Format
Provide a complete question set with:
- Clear question text
- Suggested time allocation
- Follow-up questions to probe deeper
- Brief notes on what a good answer looks like
